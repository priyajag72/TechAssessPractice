Class: Volcano

Attributes:
height (float)
radius (float)
shape (string)
volcanicStage (string)

Methods:
calculateVolume (multiplies the constant pi/3 by radius by radius by height)
changeShape (changes the shape attribute)
changeVolcanicStage (changes the volcanicStage attribute)
removeHeight (subtracts entered value from height attribute default and reassigns new value)
